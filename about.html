About Our Institution

A Foundation of Excellence & Innovation Since 1983

Established in 1985

MSPVL Polytechnic College was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between rural talent and industrial requirements. Over the years, it has stood as a beacon of technical education, shaping thousands of careers through rigorous academic standards and practical training.

Our state-of-the-art campus, equipped with the latest technology and labs, provides an ideal environment for budding engineers to innovate and excel in their respective fields.
