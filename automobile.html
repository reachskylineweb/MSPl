Department of Automobile Engineering

Accelerating towards the future of mobility with advanced service automation and design

Overview

Established in 1996, the Automobile Engineering department provides comprehensive training in production, service automation, and automotive design. We blend the features of mechanical, electronics, and software engineering to prepare students for the enormous growth in the auto industry.

Specialized Training

Our students learn through hands-on activities, including overhauling and assembling engines:

  • Dismantling, Overhauling and Assembling Engines
  • Braking, Chassis, and Steering System Maintenance
  • Autotronics & Modern Emission Control
  • Two and Three-Wheeler Technology Specialist Lab
  • Tractor and Farm Equipment Maintenance

Career Opportunities

The Automobile industry offers dynamic career paths in various roles:

  • Automotive Design & Drafting (CAD)
  • Production Supervision & Testing Units
  • Diagnostic Specialist & Service Station Manager
  • Fleet Management & Logistics Services
  • R&D in Alternative Fuels and Energy Systems
