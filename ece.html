Department of Electronics & Communication

Connecting the world through innovative circuit design and communication technology

Overview

The Electronics and Communication Engineering program covers foundational and advanced concepts of circuit design, communications, and electronic manufacturing. With the massive growth in smartphones and internet availability, we prepare students to meet consumer demands in the most economical and efficient ways.

Key Areas of Study

Our students gain industrial exposure through collaboration, internships, and expert talks in:

  • Development and Testing of Electronic Circuits
  • Aviation and Avionics Systems
  • Telecommunication & Radio/TV Systems
  • Analytical Equipment Manufacturing
  • Embedded Systems and Chip Design

Career Opportunities

ECE graduates have vast opportunities in both Telecom and Software industries:

  • Network Planning Engineer
  • Field Test Engineer
  • Customer Support Engineer
  • Electronics & Communications Consultant
  • Service Engineer
