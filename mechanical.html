Department of Mechanical Engineering

Driving industrial innovation through core engineering principles and advanced automation

Overview

Established in 1997, our Mechanical Engineering program focuses on the application of design and manufacturing principles for real-world problem-solving. We train students to analyze work using principles of motion, energy, and force to ensure efficient and reliable systems.

Key Laboratory & Workshops

Practical exposure is the core of our training in various specialized facilities:

  • Foundry and Welding Practical Workshop
  • Lathe and Drilling Practice
  • CAD/CAM & Computer-Aided Design Labs
  • Robotics & Process Automation Specialist Labs
  • Fluid Mechanics and Fluid Power Systems

Career Opportunities

Mechanical engineers remain a vital pillar of the global industrial economy:

  • Automobile & Aerospace Design Units
  • Robotics, Mechatronics & Power Plants
  • Indian Railways & Defense Infrastructure
  • Manufacturing & Production Management
  • Installation & Maintenance Services
