Department of Civil Engineering

Architecting the infrastructure of tomorrow with precision and integrity

Overview

Established in 1985, our Civil Engineering department focuses on the professional practice of designing and developing infrastructure projects. We provide students with detailed knowledge and practical skills in budgeting, surveying, and construction management.

Key Areas of Study

Our comprehensive curriculum includes advanced topics such as:

  • Structural Engineering & Theory of Structures
  • Environmental Engineering & Pollution Control
  • Soil Mechanics & Foundation Engineering
  • Remote Sensing and GIS Technology
  • Construction Management with MIS

Career Opportunities

Skilled civil engineers have immense scope in both government and private sectors:

  • National Highway Authority of India (NHAI)
  • Indian Railways & State PWD
  • Structural Design & Planning Firms
  • Steel Authority of India (SAIL)
  • Cement Corporation of India
