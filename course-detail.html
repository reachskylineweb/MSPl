Mechanical Engineering

This department focuses on the design, analysis, and maintenance of mechanical systems. We provide students with extensive hands-on experience in our state-of-the-art workshops.

Key Laboratory Facilities

  • Thermal Engineering Lab
  • Hydraulic & Pneumatic Lab
  • CNC & Robotics Center
  • Machine Shop & Fitting Shop
  • CAD/CAM Lab with Latest Software

Career Opportunities

Graduates can find opportunities in manufacturing plants, automotive industries, power plants, and government sectors like RRB, DRDO, and TNPSC.
