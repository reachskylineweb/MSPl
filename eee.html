Department of Electrical & Electronics

Mastering the power of electricity through theoretical knowledge and practical exposure

Overview

Established in 1997, the EEE department creates students with strong theoretical knowledge and extensive practical exposure. We cover essential concepts related to electricity and electric machinery, including generation, transmission, and switchgear technologies.

Key Laboratory Facilities

Our students gain hands-on experience in specialized labs including:

  • Electrical Machines-I & II Labs
  • Electronic Devices and Circuits Lab
  • Integrated Circuits & Power Electronics Lab
  • Micro Controller and Programmable Logic Controller (PLC)
  • Electrical Estimation and Energy Auditing

Career Opportunities

Electrical engineers are in high demand across multiple industries:

  • Indian Railways & Metro Rail
  • Electric Power Generating Stations
  • Automobile & Aerospace Manufacturing
  • Instrumentation & Design Engineering
  • Infrastructure & Construction Firms
