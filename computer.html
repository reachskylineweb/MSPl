Overview

Established in 2001, our department produces globally competent students committed to building a vibrant nation. We focus on training future leaders by encouraging innovative thinking and challenging existing practices in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

  • Mobile Applications & Game Design
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Robotics & Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Web Programming & Multimedia Design
